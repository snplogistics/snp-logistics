1. Project Overview

Project Name:
SNP Logistics LLC Website

Purpose

Present logistics services

Allow shippers to request quotes

Allow carriers to contact the broker

Display company credibility

Logistics companies typically provide services like truckload, LTL, air freight, warehousing, and consulting to move cargo across regions.

2. Target Users
1. Shippers

Businesses needing freight transportation.

2. Carriers

Truck owners or trucking companies looking for loads.

3. General Visitors

Checking company credibility.
